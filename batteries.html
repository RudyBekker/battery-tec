VEHICLE BATTERIES

HIGH CYCLE BATTERIES FOR CARS, TRUCKS AND MOTORCYCLES

We stock a variety of batteries for cars, motorbikes, trucks and commercial vehicles. Our range of vehicle batteries are from reputable suppliers and carry standard warranties.


FLOODED LEAD ACID - These batteries require periodic maintenance where you top up the batteries with distilled water. They have many thin lead plates inside the battery which are able to discharge large amounts of power quickly to start a vehicle engine


SEALED CALCIUM - These are sealed batteries and no maintenance is required. They also have many thin metal plates which have a special Calcium paste added to prolong the life of the battery

Vehicle batteries come in many different sizes, our team of technicians will be able to advise you on the correct type and size for your vehicle.

DEEP CYCLE BATTERIES

HEAVY DUTY DEEP CYCLE BATTERIES

When you need sustained power over longer periods of time. Our range of vehicle batteries are from reputable suppliers and carry warranties.


FLOODED LEAD ACID - Many different size options like 2V, 4V, 6V, 8V and 12V. They are robust batteries and offer a good cycle life normally around 1500 cycles at 50% DOD. These batteries require periodic maintenance. Standard 1 year warranty


SEALED CALCIUM - These are sealed batteries with fewer plates than high cycle batteries. They are able to discharge for longer periods without damaging the batteries. Cycle life of around 550 Cycles at 50% DOD. Standard 1 year warranty


ABSORBED GLASS MAT (AGM) - a sealed battery with thicker plates and a fibre mat in between each plate which absorbs electrolytes. Keeping the plates covered helps with vibration resistance. Cycle life is approximately 700 cycles @ 50% DOD. Standard 1 year warranty


GEL - a sealed battery similar to AGM but the electrolyte has a thickening agent which creates a gel which covers the plates. Cycle life is approximately 800 cycles at 50% DOD. Standard 1 year warranty

LITHIUM & SOLAR BATTERIES

HIGH QUALITY LITHIUM ION BATTERIES

We specialise in solar and battery back-up systems. The latest Lithium Ion technology pairs perfectly with these types of systems. Some reasons why Lithium Ion batteries are the ultimate deep cycle battery:


  • These batteries are designed for solar as they can discharge to 90% daily and have a fast recharge time
  • The batteries are made up of a number of Lithium cells packaged in a metal cabinet
  • You get 12v, 24v and 48v batteries
  • They are rated in kiloWatt hours (kWh)
  • Long cycle life (minimum 4000 cycles at 90% DOD)
  • They have a standard 10 year warranty


Our solar team will be able to advise you on the correct type and size of Lithium battery bank for your back-up or solar system.
