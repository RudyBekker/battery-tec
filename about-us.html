HOW IT ALL STARTED...

A PASSION FOR RENEWABLE ENERGY...

Renewable energy is something that has always been close to our hearts. We realised that through our skills and awareness of the environment it was inevitable that a solar power business would be something that would make us truly happy. Battery-Tec is a family owned business which was formed in 2012 by Clinton Barclay and his father Peter Barclay.


Battery-Tec is based in Shelly Beach on the KZN South Coast in South Africa. We have a highly-skilled and qualified team with over 50 years experience in the Electrical and Renewable Energy industries. We have partnered with some top brands in the solar, battery and leisure industry to ensure we offer the best quality and reliability to our customers.
