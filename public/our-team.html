    ABOUT US

    Renewable energy is something that has always been close to our hearts. We realised that through our skills and awareness of the environment it was inevitable that a solar power business would be something that would make us truly happy. Battery-Tec is a family owned business which was formed in 2012 by Clinton Barclay and his father Peter Barclay.

     

    Battery-Tec has a highly-skilled and qualified team with over 50 years experience in the Electrical and Renewable Energy industries. Making our clients happy is the most rewarding part of our business!

    MEET THE TEAM

    CLINTON BARCLAY (OWNER)


    Clint is a qualified electrician by trade, with many years experience in industrial, commercial & residential electrical installations. He  has found his passion in solar energy, helping customers to design and implement systems to make their energy-usage more efficient. Clint believes in a hands-on approach and is actively involved in the design & installation of all the systems supplied by Battery-Tec .

    PETER BARCLAY (OWNER)


    Peter has many years' experience in the manufacturing and customer service sectors. His passion for renewable energy and cutting-edge technology are visible in his commitment to the solar energy industry. Peter remains a committed team member and is  involved with business strategy and corporate client relations, dealing with Battery-Tec' s larger corporate and leisure customers.

    SONJA MARTIN (ACCOUNTS & ADMIN)


    If you need to know where something is ask Sonja. Sonja is our go-to team member for all our accounts, billing, ordering and administrative functions. With a background in book-keeping and accounts management Sonja keeps things running smoothly and keeps the team up-to-speed on scheduling, stock and orders. Her organisational skills ensure that the team is always on time and properly equipped.


    PAUL ELLIOTT

    ﻿(SALES MANAGER)
