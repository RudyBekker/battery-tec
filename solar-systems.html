    SOLAR OPTIONS

    We supply a comprehensive range of solar solutions from grid-tied to hybrid and full off-grid systems for residential, commercial, industrial and agricultural applications. Our systems are custom designed to suit your needs based on careful calculation and measurement of your energy needs. Solar is a great way to save money on your monthly electricity costs and to become less reliant on grid power.

    PLUG & PLAY INVERTER KITS (UPS)

    Want to have the convenience of a portable back-up power system for your home or office essentials?


    Our portable plug-and-play inverter/battery packs will do the job. Available in different sizes from 700W to 1440W versions, these systems easily link up to your home lights, TV, home theatre systems etc.


    Connected in-line these systems kick in automatically should the grid power switch off. Perfect for keeping your computer and office equipment online during power cuts or load-shedding. The inverter manages charging from grid power and automatically switches to battery power when the mains supply is interrupted.

    BATTERY BACK-UP SYSTEMS

    Similar in concept to the plug-and-play kit, just connected to your main DB board and able to handle much bigger loads.


    These systems are scalable and can be configured to run essentials such as lights, TVs, WiFi routers and networks, computers, security systems, garage doors, gate motors, water pump and many more.


    The inverter monitors grid supply and as soon as this supply is interrupted the Inverter switches to battery mode and runs all your essentials from the batteries. Everything is fully automated you won’t even know the grid power went off.


    These systems are compatible with the latest Lithium battery technology and can incorporate solar panels at a later stage.

    GRID-TIED SOLAR

    This type of system relies on solar power input (DC) during the day & grid power (AC) at night. This system is ideal for you if you want to:


    • reduce your monthly electricity costs
    • have a starter system that you can build onto 
    • reduce your reliance on the main grid (ideal for businesses operating 8am - 5pm)


    These modular systems can be customised according to your power usage patterns. The installation is linked to your main DB and includes panels, a grid-tied inverter and AC switch. Live monitoring is available for your mobile device to track  the system's performance and your savings.

    GET QUOTE

    HYBRID SOLAR

    A Hybrid system is a level above the grid-tie system. It uses a specialized inverter which has two separate inverters built into one unit - a grid-tie and battery inverter. Your home or office runs on Solar during the day and then automatically switches over to battery power at night.


    The system prioritizes solar and batteries to run your load supply but if there is a shortfall it will allow grid supply (mains) to assist.


    This system is modular meaning you can scale and build onto the system at a later stage if you wish. You can start as simply as a few panels and a battery and then scale up as you are able to afford upgrades.

    GET QUOTE

    OFF-GRID SOLAR

    Similar to a Hybrid system but with more components required. With no access to grid (mains) power you will need a back-up generator to assist during periods of poor solar production (overcast days).


    The systems are fully-automated and require larger solar arrays and battery banks to supply extended back up.


    This system is modular meaning you can scale and build onto the system at a later stage if you wish. You can start as simply as a few panels and a battery and then scale up as you are able to afford upgrades.
